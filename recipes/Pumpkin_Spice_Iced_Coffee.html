Pumkin Spice Iced Coffee

Scrumptious Coffee

This iced pumkin coffee is a perfect fall refresher

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cold brew coffee
  • ¼ cup pumpkin puree
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • 1 tablespoon sugar-free maple-flavored syrup
  • ½ cup ice, or as needed
  • 1 tablespoon whipped cream, or to taste
  • ¼ teaspoon pumpkin pie spice, divided

Steps

  1. Put cold brew, pumpkin puree, milk, syrup, and 3/4 of the pumpkin pie spice in a blender. Blend until combined and smooth.
  2. Pour into a cup with ice, add whipped cream on top, and garnish with remaining pumpkin pie spice.
